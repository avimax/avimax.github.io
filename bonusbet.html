What is the risk-free value of a bonus bet?
Answer: 65%

A $100 bonus bet, for example, can be converted into $65 cash without gambling risk. 65% is an average. Depending on the sport and availability of events, the actual percentage is usually between 60% and 70%. Breaking 80% is sometimes possible.
